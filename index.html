MY PASSION ABOUT CODING

I discovered my passion for programming after learning Java in 7th grade. Choosing biology over computer science later made me realize that coding could be my future.

During my gap year, I explored media informatics online but preferred in-person learning. Now, I’m learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP at university to pursue my passion for web development.

I’m fascinated about global connections, democracy, and AI ethics. Engaging internationally shaped my belief in empowering youth. Sports like skating and surfing keep me active and constantly growing.

I’m passionate about coding and travel. My goal is to work as a freelance web developer, exploring cultures and discovering the world.

My projects at university

Since September 2024, I’ve created hands-on multimedia projects every 1–2 weeks,
reflecting active learning and creativity during university workshops.

My group created a Scratch game
based on “Hansel and Gretel,”
teaching kids the story. I managed
the animations while players
collect keys to free Gretel.

In two weeks, we redesigned Rouen
Fine Arts Museum’s website, creating
a new wireframe on Figma and
coding the final version, also
in responsive design, ourselves.

Over three weeks, our group created the blog
"Gastro'Bordeaux," writing two articles
focused on Bordeaux’s gastronomy, local
restaurants, and the city’s sports scene.

My projects in associations

I’ve supported youth through charitable work, tutoring, and church activities. I remain active in
regional youth politics, advocating for youth voices in Bavaria to help shape our collective future.

Since 2023 I have been responsible for the
website of the umbrella organization of youth
representatives in Bavaria on WordPress,
keeping the content up to date and
communicating with the members. I support
the association in its mission to regroup
all youth councils in Germany.

First I was responsible for communication
and media, then as president of the Youth
Parliament in my home town I learnt how to
organise and hold meetings with the mayor
and work in a group of 10 people on projects
forour town for 2020-2023.
